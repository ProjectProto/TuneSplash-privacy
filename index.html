Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 19, 2026

TuneSplash is a music creation app that lets you turn humming into music. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Summary: TuneSplash does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

TuneSplash does not collect any personal information, including:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers
  • Audio recordings (your humming is processed locally and never leaves your device)

Local Data Storage

All your music projects, recordings, and settings are stored locally on your device using your browser's built-in storage (IndexedDB). This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Can be deleted by clearing your browser/app data

Microphone Access

TuneSplash requests microphone access to enable the humming-to-music feature. Audio from your microphone is:

  • Processed entirely on your device
  • Never recorded permanently unless you explicitly save a project
  • Never transmitted to any server

Internet Access

The app uses internet access solely to download instrument sound files (soundfonts) from a public CDN. No personal data is transmitted during this process.

Third-Party Services

TuneSplash does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

TuneSplash does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.
